FAQ

  • How many teams can I invite?

    You can invite a maximum of 10 teams.

  • What is the maximum file upload size?

    No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your alloted storage space.

  • How do I reset my password?

    Go to settings > personal information > reset password.

  • Can I cancel my subscription?

    You have 2 weeks every month to cancel the subscription.

  • Do you provide additional support?

    Yes our support team is always online ready to help you.
